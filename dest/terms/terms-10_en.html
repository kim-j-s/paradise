The purpose of these Paradise Signature Terms and Conditions (hereinafter “Terms and Conditions”) is to set forth matters including Paradise City’s (hereinafter “Resort”) and the members’ rights and obligations, and relevant procedures in connection with services provided to the members of Paradise Signature ( (hereinafter “Membership”) operated by the Resort.

Article 2 (Definitions)

The following terms used herein shall be defined as follows.

  • 1. The term “Paradise Signature Member” or “Member” refers to any customer having become a member pursuant to the procedure under Articles 4 and 5 hereof through the Resort’s premises or website or the Paradise City Membership Desk and authorized to use regular membership service.
  • 2. The term “Paradise Signature Card,” “Card” or, depending on the context, “Membership Card” refers to the membership card issued by the Resort to the members and approved for regular use of the membership service.
  • 3. The term “Paradise Signature Membership Benefit” or “Benefit” refers to the discounts and facility usage provided by the Resort to the members until the membership expires.
  • 4. The term “Paradise Signature Membership Coupon” or “Coupon” refers to any room voucher, meal voucher, discount voucher, etc. which can be used by the members in the Resort until the membership expires. Coupons are embedded in the membership card in the form of an electronic voucher.
  • 5. On the Resort website (hereinafter “Website”), an individual can enroll in membership, pay annual membership fees, and check available membership benefits and coupons.
  • 6. The term “Family Member” refers to an individual having become a family member by providing their personal information to the Resort and submitted a document proving the family relationship (limited to only one of the member’s direct family members), who can use the Resort-provided services and privileges until the member’s membership expires.

    • 5) If the member breaches their obligations hereunder

    • 8) If the Resort decides that it needs to refuse provision of the membership service

A member having been disqualified under Paragraph 4 above may state their opinions within 30 days of receiving the Resort’s notice, in which case the Resort shall review the matter and if it decides that the member’s positions are justified, shall take measures so that they can continue to use regular membership service.

    • 1) If a member requests cancellation of their own membership and refund of the annual membership fee, the cancellation shall become final when the Resort notifies completion of measures thereof.

Article 10 (Point Accumulation)

  • 1. The term “Point” refers to any points earned by a member through the purchase of the Resort’s goods or services or in connection with marketing activities (e.g., Resort-operated events and promotions, additional point accumulation).
  • 2. For a member having purchased the Resort’s goods or services, points shall be calculated and accumulated as follows.
  • 3. Accumulated or earned points = (Actual payment made at the time of goods or services purchase (excluding VAT)) X (Point accumulation rate, by class)
  • 4. At certain premises or leased spaces, point accumulation may not be allowed in accordance with the Resort’s policy.
  • 5. Points shall be automatically accumulated or earned on the date immediately following the payment date.
    • 1) For point accumulation, a membership card under the relevant member’s name shall be presented, in principle.
    • 2) When using the room, point accumulation varies based on membership class.
      A. RED Membership: 1% of room charge (excluding VAT)
      B. PURPLE Membership: 2% of room charge (excluding VAT)
      C. GOLD Membership: 3% of room charge (excluding VAT)
    • 3) When using the food and beverage or leisure outlets in Resort or using the premises or leased spaces in the Resort, point accumulation varies based on membership class
      A. RED Membership: 1% of the charge (excluding VAT)
      B. PURPLE Membership: 2% of the charge (excluding VAT)
      C. GOLD Membership: 3% of the charge (excluding VAT)
    • 4) Points cannot be earned when a room is booked through a travel agency or as a group.
    • 5) Points cannot be earned when a room is booked through a special contractor.
    • 6) In using the coupons/discounts provided by affiliated cards and vouchers or those provided as membership service, points are earned based on the total payment subtracted by the amount of coupons/discounts and VAT.
    • 7) Out of the points calculated or awarded under Paragraphs 2 and 4 above or additionally earned in the Resort-conducted events, decimals shall be discarded. And in the payment for the Resort’s goods and services by use of means such as points, affiliation points, gift cards, meal vouchers, room vouchers, mobile coupons, and membership coupons, not cash or credit cards, points may not be accumulated or earned.
    • 8) Points cannot be earned for the amount paid by admission tickets, vouchers, gift cards, etc. purchased through sales agents or third parties.
    • 9) A member shall request point accumulation immediately upon purchase of the Resort’s goods and services in principle, but as for their own purchase only, may request retroactive accumulation by presenting the purchase receipt within 30 days of the purchase. The Resort, under its internal policy, may change the period for requesting the retroactive accumulation, and depending on the circumstances, may refuse a member’s request for retroactive accumulation.
    • 10) Point accumulation rate and accumulation criteria are subject to changes at the discretion of the Resort in accordance with its internal policy.
    • 11) A member may check the accumulated or earned points after becoming a member and logging in to the Resort website.
    • 12) Items not eligible for point accumulation
      A. Payment to leased spaces, annual membership fee, banquet hall rental fee
      B. Meeting room rental fee, valet parking fee, cigarette, parking fee
      C. Room charge in excess of 5 rooms a day, room phone charge, and other incidental expenses to use of rooms
      D. Class fee and equipment rental fee
      E. Promotional items and purchase goods designated by the premises or the leased spaces
      F. Payment made before becoming a member
      G. Payment for special-price room packages
      H. Payment in purchases made by vouchers (gift cards, room vouchers, meal vouchers, etc.)
      I. Payment for F&B at ART HOUSE

Article 11 (Use of Points)

  • 1. In order to use points, a member shall acquire membership by applying for it and present a membership card under their name. And to use points worth a certain amount, they shall enter their password. Points accumulated or earned for a member (hereinafter “available points”) shall be spent on first-in-first-out basis.
  • 2. Minimum usable points and the unit of point use shall be designated by the Resort, and use of points shall be notified in the premises or leased spaces in one or more methods (including oral notice). Points may be used after accumulation of 3,000 points as 1 unit point each regardless of the amount of points used.
  • 3. Points may only be used in the Resort-designated premises and leased spaces.
  • 4. Points may not be transferred, loaned or used as security
  • 5. The same points may not be used by two or more persons or used multiple times, and two or more persons’ points may not be combined in payment for a single bill.
  • 6. In a member’s purchase of goods and services, discounts and points may apply simultaneously, but when other discounts are available for them, double discounts shall not be allowed and the highest discount rate shall apply.

Article 12 (Correction, cancellation and expiration of points)

  • 1. Where there is an error in a member’s accumulated or earned points, the member shall request the Resort for correction within 30 days of the occurrence of error, and the Resort shall complete the correction within three months of such request. If necessary, the Resort may request the member to present any objective evidence (e.g., receipt) which can prove the error.
  • 2. Points are valid for of 24 months from the date points are earned, and shall automatically expire monthly at the beginning of the month after such 24-month period.
  • 3. The Resort, when revoking a member’s points whose expiry date has arrived, shall notify related matters such as the points about to expire and the time thereof, by email or SMS at least 15 days in advance.
  • 4. A member may check, in the Resort website, the points about to expire and those having already expired.
  • 5. The expiry date of the points awarded by the Resort to a member as a result of marketing activities (e.g., events and promotions), not points accumulated from purchase of the Resort’s goods and services, may be separately designated in accordance with the Resort’s policies and internal conditions, and shall be separately notified to the members.
  • 6. Where the payment for a member’s purchase of goods and services is refunded, the points accumulated or used shall be canceled or restored.
